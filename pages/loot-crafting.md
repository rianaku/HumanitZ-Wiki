Typical categories:

- Gear (tools, armor, backpacks, attachments)

## <span style="color:#E79543;">2. Crafting Basics</span>

<div style="background:#111; padding:16px; border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px;">

High-level loop:

1. **Scavenge** raw materials from the world.  
2. **Refine** materials at basic crafting stations.  
3. **Assemble** gear, weapons, or structures at more advanced stations.  
4. **Maintain** weapons/gear with repair kits and spare parts.
