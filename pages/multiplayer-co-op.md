## <span style="color:#E79543;">3. Shared Base Management</span>

- Decide rules for **shared vs personal storage**.
- Use dedicated containers (weapons, meds, building mats, food).
- Agree on where to park vehicles and how to refuel.

---

## <span style="color:#E79543;">4. Co-op Tactics</span>

Bullet ideas:

- Split roles in raids (frontline, looter, rear guard).
- Use safe words/calls for retreat.
- Maintain at least one backup base or stash in case of disaster.
