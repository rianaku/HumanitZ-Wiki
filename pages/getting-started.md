## <span style="color:#E79543;">6. First Night Strategy</span>

<div style="background:#111; padding:16px; border:1px solid #333; border-radius:8px;">

Night magnifies every mistake. Before nightfall:

- Be inside a defensible building.  
- Have **some** light source.  
- Have a modest stash of food, water, and a backup weapon.  
- Know at least one safe escape route if the base is overrun.

</div>
